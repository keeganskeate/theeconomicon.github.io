About The Economicon

Our company begins with the goal to provide the most reliable business analytics for the cannabis industry. We strive to provide valued information to companies and consumers in all cannabis markets. We offer customized services at remarkebly low cost, including forecasting, price analysis, cost analysis, and consumer demand. We are also the proud publisher of The Journal of the Cannabis Industry.
